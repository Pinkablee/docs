description: "Common questions about pinkable.dev FiveM resources"
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a FiveM Keymaster license to use these resources?">
    Yes. All pinkable.dev resources are protected using FiveM's Asset Escrow
    system. Your server must run with a valid **Cfx.re Keymaster license key**.
    The license key must belong to the **same FiveM account that owns the
    purchased asset**. If the accounts do not match, the resource will fail
    to start.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why am I getting the 'missing entitlement' error?">
    This usually happens when your server license key is tied to a different
    FiveM account than the one that owns the resource. To resolve this, either
    run the server using the correct account's license key or transfer the
    asset to the account that owns the key through the
    [Keymaster asset grants page](https://keymaster.fivem.net/asset-grants).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it possible to move a purchased asset to another account?">
    Yes. FiveM allows a **single transfer per asset**. You can initiate this
    through the [Keymaster](https://keymaster.fivem.net/asset-grants) by opening
    your purchased assets list, selecting the resource, and choosing the
    transfer option. After the transfer is completed, ownership will be moved
    to the selected account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I get help if something isn't working?">
    Support is available through our official channels. You can open a support
    ticket or join the community Discord. When requesting help, please include
    useful details such as your order information, the resource name, and your
    server setup to speed up troubleshooting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are refunds available for purchases?">
    Refunds may be available depending on eligibility. Please review the
    **Refund Policy** for full details on requirements and the process for
    submitting a request.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

If you still need assistance, you can review additional troubleshooting guides
or contact our support team directly.
