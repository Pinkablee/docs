p-spawner is your gateway to an extensive vehicle spawning experience in FiveM. Discover the ease of spawning vehicles at specific markers within your server world.

**Key Features:**

- **Model Variety:** Enjoy the freedom to add a diverse range of vehicle models to your server.
- **Location Customization:** Flexibility to add and customize vehicle spawn points at your preferred locations.

**Important Note:**

You will need **[ox_lib](https://github.com/overextended/ox_lib)** as a dependency to maximize p-spawner's functionality.

**Pricing:** Free
