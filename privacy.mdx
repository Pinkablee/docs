Thank you for choosing pinkable.dev. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and providing a secure, transparent experience. - **Legal Compliance:** We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, and protect our rights.

**Security**

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please note that no security system is entirely impervious, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

**Your Choices**

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To do so or if you have concerns regarding your data, please create a support ticket in our Discord.

**Changes to this Privacy Policy**

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our information practices. Any modifications will be posted on this page along with a revised "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services following such changes will indicate your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

**Contact Us**

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please create a support ticket in our Discord.
